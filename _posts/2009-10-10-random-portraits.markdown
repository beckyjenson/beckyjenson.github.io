Here is a great example of how to make ANY location work for nice portraits.  My friend and I are actually in a parking lot of a strip mall here in Vegas for these two images.  I needed a shot to show the folks at home my new cool hair, and we needed a shot of Meg for her new [blog](http://punchfight.straubnet.net/).  We had 5 minutes, my 70-200 lens and soft evening light.  I think they turned out quite nice and you would never know we were surrounded by parked cars and chain stores...
